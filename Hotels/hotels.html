Best Non-Veg Restaurants

If you are new to Pune or if you are a foodie looking for the best non-veg restaurants in Pune, then this article is a must-read. We have shortlisted 5 best non-veg restaurants which will satisfy your cravings and hunger. As a foodie, people travel miles to eat tasty and quality food; no matter where the restaurant is located. Below some famous and best restaurants in Pune.

Guru Sawant Malvan Katta

If you are fond of “Malvani Tahli” then this place is for you. Fish lovers, this one is a must try for you.

About

It offers “Surmai Thali”, “Prawn Thali” and many more. On top of that, you will defiantly love “Solkadhi” that they offer. One of the best Seafood restaurant located at Shop No.11 A, Shree Sai Ganesh Complex, Karve Nagar Opp. TJSB Bank, Dnydeep Colony, Raghukul Society, Ganesh Nagar, Karve Nagar, Pune.

Tiranga - Satara road

A chain of outlets offers delicious non-veg food like “Kolambi Masala” and “Mutton Biryani”.

About

Price is slightly higher but I would say it’s worth every penny. The food quality and quantity is good. This is a small non-descript joint but offers tasty food it has to offer. Address: Pitambar Heights, 344, Paud Rd, Kothrud, Pune.

Hotel Jagdamba

You have to be patient if you are planning to visit this place on Saturday or Sunday. You have to wait in a queue to eat their food as too much rush is there on weekends.

About

They have varieties of thalis consist of the following items: Mutton Rasse wala, Mutton Aalni Rassa, Mutton Sukka, One Boiled Egg, Mutton Biryani with biristaa, Two Bhakri/Chapati, Unlimited Rassa

PK Biryani House

The restaurant has a cool and comfortable ambience and The menu serves North Indian and Biryani delicacies. The staff is kind and courteous which helps to make your time at PK Biryani house worth it

About

PK Biryani is loved by Punekars from long ago and people do not miss chance to go and have their Biryani. So is the case with me. I love their feast so much that I never have month when I don't visit. Talking about their Biryani, its good in taste, freshly prepared and served in good quantity!

Milan Khanawal Kumthekar Road

A nice for solo dining or casual dining with friends. It is one of the branch of milan khanawal chain spread across Pune. I prefer their sinhgad road branch as there is less crowd

About

If you are Nonveg gourmet then this place is for you. fully crowded of nonveg lovers. The food was simply awesome, must try Chicken and Mutton fry/ Masala thali. Tambda, Padhara rassa with just unbeatable taste. be there before 8 pm to avoid waiting list. must go.

SP's Biryani House

Old-fashioned biryanis and curries are served up at this fresh and simple Indian restaurant.

About

The taste of the food follows their reputation. Very amazing, lip smacking food. They have a large menu, and it can be confusing if you are not sure of what you want to eat. Personal recommendations from the staff is available. We did order the Chicken specialty along with the Chicken dum biryani and both the dishes were really really awesome. The service is very helpful and attentive. Would love to come back again.

Siddhivinayak Dining Hall

This place is for people who love eating puneri food. The restaurant ambiance is good and clean. The staff and owner are also kind. The food tastes awesome the favourite is Dhai Vada. The price are also moderate Monday To Saturday - 150/- Unlimited SPL on Sunday - 250/- Unlimited

About

Thali served is here simple, not very spicy or oily. Menu is simple and easy to digest. Sweets is extra if you want. Budget friendly stomachful meals ! The only problems is in rush hours waiting area is having limited sitting arrangements and hence people have to wait in standing.

Durvankur Dining Hall

Good place to have a unlimited thali. Variety of dishes. Mainly variety of sweet dishes. 380 rs per person (on the date of review) If you're a foodie and can consume a lot then it's a heaven for you with value for money. But if you're don't eat much then maybe it's waste of your money.(you can try once). Overall nice experience, but there is no value for money. More sweet dishes.

About

Visited this place for family get together.. its a dining hall which has fixed Maharashtrian menus like usal,mugachi khichdi,thalipith etc. They start serving as soon as you reache to the table. You don't have any a-la carte option. This thali is unlimited. food taste was good. Must try their Dahiwada.As its dining hall so don't expect much interior like other restaurants. Service was really very quick. Cost for per thali is Rs.250. Good place those love unlimited thali n have capacity to eat it.

Phadke dining Hall

You can have homely taste here. Good atmosphere good vibes Price is little high for this type of Thali but everything is unlimited. Service is very much instant. They close at exactly 10 p.m.

About

Phadke Dining Hall is a place close to everyone's heart. Famous for authentic Marathi food. The place is great for family get togethers. The staff is friendly and they don't just rush to serve the food. They also have four wheeler parking in house.

Asha Dining Hall

Nice place for having simple lunch. They serve rice plate and also have sweet dishes as optional. Food taste was good and was very simple preparations. It's located on first floor and no lift so people having walking issues should talk care.

About

It is located inside a residential complex. Famous for authentic Maharashtrian thali. The food is simple, nonspicy yet delicious. Tastes just like home-cooked food. Sweets are available at extra cost.

Hotel Shreyas

They have a Thali system and the food was delicious. We went on a weekend so there was waiting. Once you are in, the staff will serve you quickly and will regularly check up on you,if you need anything extra. Overall the experience was an excellent one!

About

Loved the menu like, sabudana wada, crispy bhajjis, puris, masale bhat, only basundi which is not included in thali charges. And in the end that simple masala Pan wow it melts in the mouth

Atithi Veg

Good place to visit with family, friends etc. Food quality is good although can't say the same regarding quantity. There is a lot of waiting approximately 40 mins, but as the hotel is huge they are able to accommodate everyone although it seems the crowd is huge. Overall good experience and some what value for money, definitely visit one time when nearby

About

The service is neither fast nor slow. Has a very elaborate menu and all dishes taste different wrt each other. They add too much garlic to everything. And everything was a little spicy... Our serving instructions were followed well. Must try veg crispy rice soup. The place is large and is usually crowded. There's ample parking space here. Little expensive dining though

Popular Food Restaurants

Roopali Hotel

If you are hungry after gazing at Saras Garden, come to this restaurant to have a meal. Taste Indian and Vegetarian cuisines here. Try nicely cooked idli, masala dosa and vada. Come here and order good pancakes and tasty ice cream. Delicious bitter is the best drinks of Roopali Hotel. It's nice to order great cold coffee, tea or lemonade.

About

Most guests indicate that the waitstaff is efficient. If you want to enjoy spectacular service, you should visit this place. The designer created a lovely decor. This spot has got Google 4.1 according to the visitors' opinions.

Vaishali Restaurant

After a walk around Saras Garden, many visitors stop by this restaurant. Immerse yourself in stunning Indian cuisine here. Try perfectly cooked masala dosa, idli and sambar. You will enjoy its food, particularly tasty pancakes, ice cream and kulfi. Come to Vaishali for delicious cordial, wine or bitter. Based on the guests' opinions, waiters offer good cold coffee, masala chai or fresh juices.

About

Food delivery is a big plus of this place. The professional staff at this spot can demonstrate how much they like their clients. The fine service is something these restaurateurs care about. Prices are found fair here. At this restaurant, people can enjoy the comfortable ambiance and spectacular decor. As for the Google rating, Vaishali received 4.3.

Wadeshwar

After enjoying Saras Garden, it may be a good idea to visit this restaurant. Clients highlight that they like the menu of Indian and Vegetarian cuisines here. The recipe for success of Wadeshwar is its tasty idli, vada and chole bhature. A number of reviewers find that you can have good pancakes, kulfi and gulab jamun here. This place is famous for delicious bitter. Get your meal started with great lassi, masala chai or cold coffee.

About

The energetic staff reflects the style and character of this place. Spectacular service is something guests agree upon here. According to the guests' opinions, the decor can be described as cute. Google users awarded this spot 4.2.
